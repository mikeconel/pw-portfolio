About This Qualification

The Certified Practitioner in Specialist Property Finance (CPSP) is an advanced LIBF qualification demonstrating expertise in complex, non-standard property finance products that fall outside mainstream mortgage advice.

This qualification enables me to advise on the full spectrum of specialist property finance solutions for investors, developers and business owners with complex property requirements.
