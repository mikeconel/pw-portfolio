About This Qualification

The Level 4 Diploma for Professional Financial Advisers from the Chartered Banker Institute covers the full suite of R01–R06 examinations mandated by the FCA for retail investment advisers.

Completion of this diploma demonstrates the minimum competence standard required by the FCA to provide regulated investment advice to retail clients in the UK.

About This Qualification

R01–R06 are the FCA's mandated examination units for retail investment advisers under the Retail Distribution Review (RDR).

Awarded by the Chartered Banker Institute, a globally recognised banking and finance professional body.
