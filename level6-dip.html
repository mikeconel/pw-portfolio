About This Qualification

The Level 6 Diploma in Advanced Financial Advice (DipAFA) is LIBF's flagship advanced qualification. It is recognised as a QCF Level 6 qualification — equivalent to a bachelor's degree — and forms the academic route to Chartered Financial Planner status.

Achieving this qualification demonstrates mastery of complex financial planning across pensions, investments, protection, estate planning and regulatory compliance.

About the DipAFA

The DipAFA is recognised as part of the Chartered Financial Planner pathway via The London Foundation for Banking & Finance (LIBF).

Equivalent to Level 6 on the Regulated Qualifications Framework (RQF).
