About This Qualification

The Certificate in Mortgage Advice and Practice (CeMAP) is the industry-standard qualification for mortgage advisers in the UK, awarded by the London Institute of Banking & Finance (LIBF).

Holding CeMAP enables me to provide regulated mortgage advice to clients, including residential purchases, remortgages, buy-to-let and specialist mortgage products.

About CeMAP

CeMAP is recognised by the FCA as meeting the mortgage adviser qualification standard under the Mortgage Credit Directive.

Also holds the Certified Mortgage Adviser designation from LIBF, recognising ongoing professional practice.
