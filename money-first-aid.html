About This Qualification

Money First Aid is a UK-based organisation training practitioners to become Certified Money First Aiders® — professionals equipped to identify and respond to clients experiencing financial difficulty, stress or crisis.

Holding this certification complements formal financial advice by ensuring I can support clients not just with their investments and pensions, but with their overall financial wellbeing and resilience.
