About This Qualification

The Certificate in Regulated Equity Release from LIBF qualifies me to advise clients on FCA-regulated equity release products, including lifetime mortgages and home reversion plans.

This is a specialist area of later-life lending where careful suitability assessment is critical — I ensure all clients fully understand the long-term implications and explore all available alternatives.

About Equity Release Advice

Equity release is regulated by the FCA under MCOB rules. Only advisers holding this specialist qualification may provide equity release advice.

All products recommended must meet Equity Release Council standards, including the no-negative-equity guarantee.
